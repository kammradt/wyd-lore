## Article Summary

According to the [Wikipedia article](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/With_Your_Destiny), **With Your Destiny (WYD)** is:

- A Free-to-play Scandinavian-based MMORPG
- Developed by JoyImpact Co., Ltd.
- Originally published by HanbitSoft Inc. in May 2003
- Also known as "Supreme Destiny" in Malaysia and the Philippines
- Currently published globally by Raid Hut (licensed in February 2019)
- Features a mobile version "WYD M" released in 2025

### Key Information

**Publisher History:**
- Original: HanbitSoft Inc. (2003)
- Vibrant Communications Ltd. (2008-2019)
- Current: Raid Hut Ltd. (2019-present, global) and JoyImpact (South Korea)

**Game Features:**
- 4 Custom Classes: Transknight, Hunter, Foema, and Beastmaster
- Over 1500 levels of progression (Mortal to Supreme Celestial)
- Castle Wars supporting up to 1000 simultaneous players
- Integrated macro system for automation
- 150+ skills with customizable builds
- Guild ranking and PVP systems

**Lore:**
The game centers around the Supreme Beings Yetzirah and Tzfah who created the continent of Kersef, where two rival kingdoms (Hekalotia and Akeronia) battle for dominion while facing threats from divine forces.
