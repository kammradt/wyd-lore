**With Your Destiny**, often referred to as WYD, is a [Free-to-play](/wiki/Free-to-play "Free-to-play") [Scandinavian](/wiki/Scandinavia "Scandinavia")-based [MMORPG](/wiki/MMORPG "MMORPG") developed by JoyImpact Co., Ltd.,[[1]](#cite_note-1) and was published and marketed worldwide by [HanbitSoft Inc.](/wiki/HanbitSoft "HanbitSoft") in May 2003.[[2]](#cite_note-2) It is also known as **Supreme Destiny** in Malaysia and the Philippines.[[3]](#cite_note-3)[[4]](#cite_note-4) In 2025, WYD M, the game's mobile version, was released.

## Publisher

[[edit](/w/index.php?title=With_Your_Destiny&action=edit&section=1 "Edit section: Publisher")]

In February 2019, JoyImpact licensed Raid Hut, a Brazilian [game publisher](/wiki/Video_game_publisher "Video game publisher"),[[5]](#cite_note-5) as its sole official global publisher[[6]](#cite_note-:0-6)[[7]](#cite_note-7)[[8]](#cite_note-:2-8) and terminated the contract with Vibrant Games due to [breaches](/wiki/Breach_of_contract "Breach of contract").[[8]](#cite_note-:2-8) JoyImpact also publishes the game, which has a South Korea-based server,[[9]](#cite_note-9) and started planning to remake the game from Q1 2019, including updated graphics, new class, and contents.[[8]](#cite_note-:2-8) Raid Hut is taking action on [game piracy](/wiki/Video_game_piracy "Video game piracy") by [taking down](/wiki/Notice_and_take_down "Notice and take down") the [websites](/wiki/Website "Website") of illegal servers ([private servers](/wiki/Private_server "Private server")) on [Google search](/wiki/Google_Search "Google Search") and disabling their [social media websites](/wiki/Social_media "Social media").[[6]](#cite_note-:0-6)

## Plot

[[edit](/w/index.php?title=With_Your_Destiny&action=edit&section=2 "Edit section: Plot")]

In the story of With Your Destiny,[[10]](#cite_note-:1-10) the Supreme Beings Yetzirah and Tzfah joined forces to create a world where mankind could triumph. In this plan, two great rival kingdoms disputed the dominion of the continent of Kersef: Hekalotia and Akeronia. While the realms were distracted by endless battles, a confrontation between the deities threatened to annihilate all Kersef. In this raid, players must master new skills and weapons and form powerful guilds to face the greatest of all threats: the fury of two supreme gods.

## Gameplay

[[edit](/w/index.php?title=With_Your_Destiny&action=edit&section=3 "Edit section: Gameplay")]

With Your Destiny has various modes of wars, divided into three:[[10]](#cite_note-:1-10)

* Castle Wars: All players can participate with their respective guilds, form alliances, and choose the attack tactics in a war of up to 1000 simultaneous players.
* City Conquests: Groups of 26 vs. 26 dispute the dominance of the main cities of the game.
* Battle of Kingdoms: similar to a MOBA, each team must make its way to victory facing the challenges of the map, in addition to the Tournaments between players.

Key systems include:[[10]](#cite_note-:1-10)

* Integrated [macro system](/wiki/Macro_%28computer_science%29 "Macro (computer science)"): Automated attack, skill, and hunting functions.
* Infinite progression: Over 1500 levels to reach, players can evolve from a mortal to a supreme celestial being.
* 4 Custom Classes (Characters): Transknight, Hunter, Foema, and Beastmaster.
* Highly customizable builds: Players can create strategies, combining attributes, and more than 150 skills.
* [PVP](/wiki/Player_versus_player "Player versus player"): Several competitive modes of up to 1000 players.
* Craft and Trade system: Players can loot items and trade with other players, including mounts.
* Guild Ranking System: Guilds can fight each other to climb the ranks and dominate the server.
* Solo or Party gameplay: Players can play alone through quests, dungeons, and activities or create a party to fight monsters and receive better rewards.
